About Camp Chita

Where conservation meets comfort.
Nestled in the heart of Zambia’s breathtaking wilderness, Camp Chita is more than a campsite — it’s a sanctuary where nature, adventure, and community intertwine. Our eco‑tourism philosophy celebrates harmony between sustainability and serenity, offering guests an experience that’s both rejuvenating and responsible.

A Place to Belong

Whether you’re drawn by the thrill of exploration or the calm of the outdoors, Camp Chita provides a haven for every kind of traveler. Wake up to birdsong echoing through the valley, spend your days discovering hidden trails and river bends, and unwind under a canopy of stars beside a crackling campfire.

Experiences That Connect

From guided hikes and river adventures to cultural storytelling and wildlife encounters, every moment here is designed to immerse you in Zambia’s natural rhythm. Our team’s commitment to conservation ensures that your stay not only connects you with the land but also helps preserve it for generations to come.

Our Promise

At Camp Chita, you don’t just visit nature — you become part of it.
