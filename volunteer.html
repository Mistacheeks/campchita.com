Why Volunteer

Be Part Of Something Meaningful

Volunteering at Camp Chita is more than an outdoor experience — it is an opportunity to contribute to environmental conservation, sustainable tourism, and community empowerment in one of Zambia’s most inspiring natural settings.

Whether you are passionate about wildlife, environmental education, conservation, photography, youth mentorship, or outdoor leadership, your contribution helps preserve ecosystems while creating positive impact for local communities.

Volunteer Opportunities

Explore Volunteer Roles

Our volunteer opportunities combine conservation, education, outdoor adventure, and community engagement to create impactful and unforgettable experiences.

Wildlife Conservation

Assist with wildlife monitoring, conservation awareness, biodiversity recording, and habitat protection initiatives across the Camp Chita ecosystem.

Environmental Education

Support eco-education programs, school outreach activities, and youth environmental leadership initiatives within surrounding communities.

Media & Storytelling

Help document conservation efforts through photography, videography, content creation, and storytelling campaigns for awareness and tourism promotion.
